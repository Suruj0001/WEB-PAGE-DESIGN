    About ISRO

    India decided to go to space when Indian National Commitee

    for Indian National Commitee for Space Research (INCOSPAR) was set up by the Government of India in 1962.

    With the Visionary Dr Vikram Sarabhai at its helm, INCOSPAR setr up the Thumba Equatorial Rocket

    Launching Station (TERLS) in Thiruvanthapuram for upper atmospheric research


    Indian Space Research Organisation, formed in 1969, superseded the erstwhile INCOSPAR

    Vikram sarabhai having, having identified the role and importance

    of space technology in a Nation Development provided ISRO the neccesssary direction to function

    as an agent of development ISRO the neccessary direction to function as ana agent of development .

    ISRO then embarked on its mission to provide the nation space Based services and to develop the technologies to achieve the same independently
